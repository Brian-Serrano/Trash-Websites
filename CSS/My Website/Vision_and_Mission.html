Vision and Mission

Vision

We envision to holistically develop a Filipino learner with 21st century skills which caters to the needs of national and global community through continuous improvement.


Mission

We members of the Cabugao Institute family commit ourselves to protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture based and complete basic education where:

  • Students learn in a child-friendly, gender sensitive, safe and motivating environment.
  • Teachers facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner.
  • Administrators and staffs as stewards of the institution ensure and enabling and supportive environment for effective learning to happen.
  • Family, community and other stakeholders are actively engaged and share responsibility for developing life-long learners.
