CI Hymn

Hail! Cabugao Institute
Gem so pure and true
Loyal sons ye follow
Where our dreams come true.

Noble deeds we do
Night and day they glow
To enchant us with delight
And to know to love the right.

CI, beloved, faithfully united
Onward we all march on
Forward we reach our goal
Praise love and honor
We bring them all.

Thou art the guiding star
The light seen near and far
CI thy name shall live forever
Within the hearts of all.
